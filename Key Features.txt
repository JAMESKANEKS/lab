Key Features
Service Availability List
Displays all laboratory services (e.g., X-ray, blood tests, diagnostic scans).
Shows service name, category, and current price.
Indicates if a service is available or temporarily unavailable.
Daily Transaction Log
Shows all services availed by patients for the day.
Auto-generates:
Quantity of services availed
List of patients and services
*Total amount collected for the day
Date Selection & Report Recovery
Allows staff to select a  specific date  to view past transactions.
Retrieves:
Services availed on that date
Total income
Number of transactions
Useful for generating weekly or monthly reports.
Receipt Printing
Automatically generates receipts containing:
Laboratory name & contact details
Patient name
List of availed services
Individual prices
Grand total
Date & time of transaction
Receipts may be printed or saved as PDF.
Price & Service Management
Admin can update:
Price per service
Add or remove items
Mark services as available/unavailable
Secure Staff Access
Multiple user roles (Admin, Cashier, Laboratory Staff).
Tracks edits and transaction history for security.
